DPS8M Simulator
===============

DPS8M is a simulator of the 36‑bit GE Large Systems / Honeywell /
Bull 600/6000‑series mainframe computers (Honeywell 6180, Honeywell
Series‑60 ∕ Level‑68, and Honeywell ∕ Bull DPS‑8/M) descended from
the GE‑645 and engineered to support the Multics operating system.

DPS8M is open source software developed by The DPS8M Development Team
and many contributors.

For more information, visit our website at https://dps8m.gitlab.io/ or
review the DPS8M Omnibus Documentation PDF.
